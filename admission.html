Admission Process

Simple Steps to Get Started

  1. 1

    Fill Enquiry Form

    Share your details via the form on this page, or visit us at Sindhu Nagar, Bhilwara for a walk-in free counselling session.

  2. 2

    Counselling & Course Selection

    Our expert team guides you to choose the right course based on your career goals and educational background.

  3. 3

    Document Submission

    Submit Aadhar card, 10th marksheet & 2 passport photos for registration and official enrollment.

  4. 4

    Fee Payment & Batch Allotment

    Pay the course fee and receive your batch timing. Classes begin within 2–3 days of enrollment.

  5. 5

    Start Learning! 🎉

    Attend classes, practice daily in our lab, take mock tests, and earn your government-recognized certificate.
